Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the construction process typically take?

Timeline varies by project scope. A residential home typically takes 6-18 months, while commercial projects may span 12-36 months. We provide detailed project schedules during the proposal phase.

What payment terms do you offer?

We offer flexible payment schedules tied to project milestones. Typical terms are 30% upfront, 40% on start work, and 30% on completion. Custom arrangements can be negotiated.

Do you offer warranty on completed projects?

Yes. We provide a 2-year defect warranty on all structural and MEP works, and 5-year on major building systems. Extended warranties available upon request.

Can you work within tight budgets and timelines?

Absolutely. Our experience managing multiple projects allows us to optimize resources and timelines. We'll work with you to establish realistic targets and manage expectations from day one.
