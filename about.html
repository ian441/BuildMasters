About BuildMaster

Our Story, Values, and Commitment to Excellence

Our Journey

Building Trust for
Two Decades

BuildMaster was founded in 2005 with a simple vision: to build structures that last and relationships that endure. What started as a small team of dedicated tradespeople has grown into one of East Africa's most respected construction firms.

Over 20 years, we've completed over 350 projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. Every project — whether a family home on Karen Estate or a 24-storey commercial tower in the CBD — receives the same commitment to quality, safety, and client satisfaction.

Today, our team of engineers, architects, and skilled craftspeople bring expertise, accountability, and innovation to every build. We don't just construct buildings; we build legacies.

Our Evolution

Company Timeline

2005
Founded
BuildMaster is established by a group of senior builders and engineers with a vision to fill a gap in the market for honest, quality-driven construction services.
2008
First Major Commercial Project
Completed a 12-storey commercial office complex on time and within budget, establishing our reputation in the commercial sector.
2012
NCA Grade 8 Certification
Achieved NCA Grade 8 contractor status, opening doors to larger public and private sector projects.
2015
100+ Projects Milestone
Celebrated completion of 100 residential and commercial projects with a zero LTI (Lost Time Injury) record.
2018
Regional Expansion
Added offices in Uganda and Tanzania to serve East African clients with consistent quality and local expertise.
2021
250+ Projects Completed
Reached 250+ completed projects and established ourselves as the go-to construction partner for major developments.
2025
20 Years of Excellence
After two decades, we celebrate 350+ completed projects, 280+ satisfied clients, and continue our mission to build lasting value.
